Trusted Servants

The members who serve our group in various capacities

Group Service Positions

Our group is self-supporting through the service of our members. The following positions are currently held by trusted servants:

Group Secretary

Responsible for maintaining group records and communications

Treasurer

Manages group finances and 7th Tradition contributions

Meeting Chair

Facilitates daily meetings and ensures smooth operation

Tech Coordinator

Manages online meeting platform and technical support

Service Opportunities

We welcome members who wish to serve. Service positions are rotated regularly to give everyone an opportunity to participate in the group's functioning.

If you're interested in serving, please speak with any current trusted servant or contact us at awakeningnstulsa@gmail.com.
